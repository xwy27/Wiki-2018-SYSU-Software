XMU China
Overview
As a software team, we felt obliged to help other experimental team with our expertise in website making and data processing. At the same time, we need to learn need and suggestion from experimental team to develop our software. Thus, we actively participated in collaboration with other teams, and reach out for 4 registered iGEM team: XMU-China, SCUT-ChinaA, WHU-China and SYSU-CHina.
XMU-China
We met XMU-China at September in Guang Zhou. We had a meetup and exchange our project.They want to get more information and experience to optimize their subject, and asking if there is a software which can help them find past iGEM subjects out of their need quickly and accurately.
Integrated with the need of XMU-china and our survey in laboratory, we find it is important for synthetic biologists to get past successful experience quickly and accurately to optimize their own subjects, which can also be valuable to inspire users in the process of collaboration. To this end, we build a database storing iGEM subjects and paper works, and develop a search engine for users index this information easily and quickly.
After development, we provided XMU-China with our software to help us test it and satisfied their need of searching. They found that the searching results were perfectly matching their pervious information.
For more details check XMU-China.

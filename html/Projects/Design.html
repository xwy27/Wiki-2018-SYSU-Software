Overview
Real-time collaboration like face to face
CO-RAD isn’t just another question-and-answer community. It is an amazing experience specially made for synthetic biologists. Our real-time collaborative designer and Shared sketchpad will make you feel like working side by side with your remote coworkers. The powerful databases and algorithms we provide empowers you by bringing out your creativity, and you will find yourself doing a better job for your team in no time.
effective tools + valuable content = good cooperation
Real-time Collaborative Designer
If you have a brilliant idea that you want to share with your colleagues right now, wouldn’t it be best if they can see it as you are writing it down? With our CO-RAD online design platform, team members can see each other’s edits on the file in real time, as if they are using the same computer with multiple screens and input devices. You can also customize new tools for the platform for higher efficiency when you are editing relations between genetic circuits or writing experiment protocols. CO-RAD online design platform also comes with version control, so you don’t ever need to worry about someone accidentally scrambling your files up!
Shared Sketchpad
It works like our Real-time Collaborative designer, but instead of making edits on test files or editing circuits, you get to doodle on a synchronization board and other people can see it as you draw and respond to you at the same time. Let ideas fly!
SBOL Support
If you have work on SBOL files on other platforms, you can use those files in our software without any re-editing or configuration. This allows you to collaborate seamlessly with people who are not currently using the same software as you.
Help Users Come Up With More Valuable Ideas
Good collaboration requires good advice, and a good proposal cannot be separated from a summary of past successful experience, a deep understanding of existing designs. To this end, we have developed several functions, hoping to enlighten users' inspiration and open up users' thinking.
Search
Each year hundreds of teams participate in iGEM and lots of synthetic biology papers are published. By learning from successful examples from the past, you can refine your own project or make valuable suggestions for others’ work. Our database include former iGEM and well-known journal projects which can be searched by keywords.
Recommendation
Our software will auto-recommend project that have similar genetic circuits as user’s current project.
Analysis
CO-RAD computes CG and CAI, which are important values for a circuit. These values can help users detect possible optimization potentials.
Simulation and evolutionary algorithm
CO-RAD can build mathematical models based on the circuit you have designed. It can give you an idea of the protein expression of the DNA. We can help you perfect your design using an algorithm that simulate the determinate evolution process. All you need to do is specify the goal protein expression, and the software will give you the best parameters via a simulation process of reproduction, biological elimination, and mutation. These parameters will be helpful for you to optimize the promoter, RBS intensity, or DNA sequence.
